How it works

Four steps, running continuously in the background

You give Flintel your website. It does the reading, the searching, and the scoring — you just review what it finds.

01

Read your site

Flintel reads your homepage and key pages to work out who buys from you, what they're frustrated by, and who they're comparing you against.

02

Scan for threads

It continuously watches Reddit for threads that match those buyer questions — and checks which of them already rank on Google.

03

Match to your keywords

Every thread is matched against the discovery keywords from your report, so your dashboard only ever shows what's relevant to you.

04

Track and review

New matches land in your signal feed as they're found. Mark the ones you've reviewed and keep an eye on competitor mention volume.
