01 · How it works

Real analysis. Real matching. No fabricated numbers.

Claude reads your site and figures out how buyers talk about your problem. Flintel matches that against already-tracked Reddit/Twitter signals, and a live background job keeps matching new ones — even while you're logged out.

You see only what actually matched.

No placeholder counts. If your site only matches 3 threads today, the report shows 3 — and grows as the tracker finds more.

Talking points, not fake comments.

Flintel never writes an undisclosed "customer story." It gives your team notes to write their own, disclosed reply.

Nothing is posted or upvoted for you.

No engagement manipulation, ever. You stay fully in control of what goes where.
